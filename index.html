Background

The path here wasn't linear. I've sailed solo to Hawaii and back, thru-hiked a thousand miles of the Appalachian Trail, and worked my way from deckhand to ferry captain in under a year. I got hired as a sailing instructor when I showed up to take lessons, eventually becoming a senior instructor with teaching awards.

Along the way I picked up dive certifications from Open Water through Divemaster, led expeditions in Indonesia, supervised teams at Burning Man, and served as Commodore of the Berkeley Yacht Club. Each experience taught me something about systems, leadership, and what it takes to make things work.

Now I build software that solves real problems - the kind I've encountered firsthand. The same attention to detail that keeps a boat safe at sea goes into every line of code.
