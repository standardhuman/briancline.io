Background

For nearly 20 years I've taught sailing on San Francisco Bay. Docking, offshore passage making, everything in between. The part that lights me up is watching someone go from white-knuckled to confident in the space of an afternoon. Breaking complex skills into steps someone can actually use.

I build the same way. A marketplace that connects boat owners with trusted service providers. A guided practice for working through conflict with compassion. A mobile app that turns a 60-second voice memo into a professional invoice. Different tools, same job: make the hard thing approachable.

I'm also drawn to long distances. Solo sailing to Hawaii. A thousand miles on the Appalachian Trail. Cross-country motorcycling. Long mountain bike rides through Marin and Southern Utah. Sustained effort over time strips away everything that doesn't matter.
