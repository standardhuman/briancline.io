Wireless Coverage Map



The map below shows our wireless coverage area. Theoretically anything in the green areas can be covered by at least one of our towers, so long as their are no buildings or foliage blocking your view. If you are in the green area and have a good view of the East Bay ridgeline, the ridgeline above Sausalito, Sutro Tower, or Mt. Diablo you should be in coverage. The areas that are in the grey area are not covered. If you are on a border of the grey area please do not hesitate to contact us, we will run your address and get back to you as soon as possible, usually within the same business day.
